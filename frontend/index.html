How it works

Step 1: Search for a tutor

Use our smart search feature to
find tutors based on subject,
tutor name or location. Filter by
availablity, price range, and
reviews to match your needs.




Step 2: Book your tutor

Check tutor profiles for details
like expertise, hourly rate, and
reviews. Select a date and time
that fits your schedule and book
directly through our platform



Step 3: Learn Online

Join the session using our built-
in video platform with tools like
screen sharing, a virtual
whiteboard, and file sharing.
Enjoy an engaging and
interactive learning experience!






Step 4: Achieve Your Goals

Master new skills, improve grades,
and build confidence. Rate your
tutor and schedule future sessions
as needed!
